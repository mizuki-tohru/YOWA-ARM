Guidance of processing:

This is data of the optical encoder's codewheel of a through slits incremental quadrature type encoder.
To encorder_org_23.dxf which is a reflective slits, you paste together and create encorder_org_24.dxf of a transparent material.
I used the cutting plotter, Silhouette-Cameo for creation.
It reduce data to 1/6 after reading. Please reduce to 46% next after reducing to 36% first.

cutting parameter:
encorder_org_23.dxf
Material: Film label seal Silver thickness 0.07mm 
parameter: speed 4 thickness 7 Cutter 5 

encorder_org_24.dxf
 vinyl chloride board thickness 0.3mm
parameter: speed 1 thickness 24 Cutter 8
